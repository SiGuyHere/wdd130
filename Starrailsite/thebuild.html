The Builds

Here is where we will go over the builds for each character, showing the relic sets and light cones that you want to build. We will show the top two 5 star and 4 star light cones for the character, as well as the top two relic sets.

Quantum Imaginary Link 3

Seele's Build

For Seele’s Lightcones, 1. In the Night, 2.Sleep Like the Dead. For her Relics, 1. 4PC Genius of Brilliant Stars, 2. 2PC Genius of Brilliant Stars + 2PC Musketeer of Wild Wheat. For her Planar Ornaments, 1. Rutilant Arena, 2. Space Sealing Station. On the Body you will want crit rate or crit damage, on the Boots you will want ATK%, on the Sphere you will want Quantum DMG%, and on the Rope you will want ATK%. The best sub stats for her are crit rate/crit damage, ATK%, and speed. For Silver Wolf’s Lightcones, 1. Before the Tutorial Mission Starts, 2. Incessant Rain. For her Relics, 1. 4PC Genius of Brilliant Stars, 2. 4PC Thief of Shooting Meteor. For her Planar Ornaments, 1. Sprightly Vonwacq, 2. Inert Salsotto. On the Body you will want EHR%, on the Boots you will want SPD, on the Sphere you will want either HP% or DEF%, and on the Rope you will want either BE% or Energy Reg. The best sub stats for her are EHR%, speed, BE%, and ATK%.

Penacony, Land of dreams, 2. Sprightly Vonwacq. The best sub stats for her are EHR%, Speed, BE%, and ATK%.

Fu Xuan's Build

For Fu Xuan’s Lightcones, 1. She Already Shut Her Eyes, 2. Moment of Victory. For her Relics, 2-PC: Longevous Disciple / Guard of Wuthering Snow / Messenger Traversing Hackerspace / Knight of Purity Palace. For her Planar Ornaments, 1. Fleet of the Ageless, 2. Penacony, Land of the Dreams. On the Body, you will want HP% or DEF%, on the Boots, you will want Speed, on the Sphere, you will want HP% or DEF%, and on the Rope, you will want ERR% or HP%. The best sub stats for her are HP% or DEF%, Speed, DEF%, and Effect Resistance.
