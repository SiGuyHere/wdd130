How To Use

Welcome to the How to Use page. On this page we will be going over what each character does, their roles in team comps, as well as how strong they are overall, with a rank of C, B, A, and S. We will be assuming you have a level 80, E0 Character.

Seele's Abilities

Seele is a A rank damage dealer. Seele's abilities revolve around dealing Quantum damage and utilizing a buffed state. Her basic attack inflicts Quantum damage equal to 50% of her ATK to a single enemy. The skill increases Seele's SPD by 25% for 2 turns and deals Quantum damage equal to 110% of her ATK to a single enemy. Upon activating her ultimate, Seele enters a buffed state, dealing Quantum damage equal to 255% of her ATK to a single enemy. Her talent, "Resurgence," triggers when defeating an enemy with Basic ATK, Skill, or Ultimate, granting an extra turn with a 40% increased damage for one turn. Notably, enemies defeated in the extra turn won't trigger another "Resurgence." Additionally, her technique provides Seele with Stealth for 20 seconds, rendering her undetectable by enemies, and entering the buffed state upon initiating battle by attacking enemies.

Silver Wolf's Abilities

Silver Wolf is a S rank debuffer. Silver Wolf possesses a versatile set of abilities, including a basic attack that deals Quantum damage equal to 50% of her ATK to a single enemy. Her skill has a 75% base chance to add a Weakness of an on-field ally's Type to the target, reducing the enemy's DMG RES by 20% for 2 turns, and a 100% chance to reduce All-Type RES by 7.5% for 2 turns, while dealing Quantum damage equal to 98% of her ATK. The ultimate ability carries an 85% base chance to decrease the target's DEF by 36% for 3 turns and deals Quantum damage equal to 228% of her ATK. Additionally, Silver Wolf's talent allows her to create Bugs, reducing ATK by 5%, DEF by 4%, and SPD by 3%, with a 60% chance to implant a random Bug lasting 3 turns with each attack. Her technique involves an immediate attack, dealing Quantum damage equal to 80.0% of her ATK to all enemies, ignoring Weakness Types, and triggering the Quantum Weakness Break effect for enemies with their Weakness Broken.

Fu Xuan's Abilities

Fu Xuan is a S rank sustain character. Fu Xuan's abilities comprise a unique skill set. In her basic attack, she deals Quantum damage equal to 25% of her Max HP to a single enemy. Her skill activates the Matrix of Prescience, causing other team members to distribute 65% of the damage they receive to Fu Xuan for 3 turns. During this time, all allies gain the Knowledge effect, increasing their Max HP by 3% of Fu Xuan's Max HP and boosting their CRIT Rate by 6%. The effect ends when Fu Xuan is knocked down. Fu Xuan's ultimate deals Quantum damage equal to 60% of her Max HP to all enemies and triggers the HP Restore effect. Her talent, while she is active in battle, provides Misfortune Avoidance to the entire team, reducing damage taken by 10%. When Fu Xuan's HP falls to 50% or less of her Max HP, it triggers HP Restore, restoring 80% of the missing HP, with a maximum of 2 trigger counts. However, it cannot be triggered if Fu Xuan receives a killing blow. Finally, her technique grants a 20-second Barrier to all team members, blocking enemy attacks and preventing the team from entering battle when attacked. Entering battle while the Barrier is active automatically activates Matrix of Prescience for 2 turns.

Welt's Abilities

Welt is a B rank debuffer. Welt's skill set is marked by powerful Imaginary damage abilities. His basic attack deals Imaginary damage equal to 50% of his ATK to a single enemy. The skill inflicts Imaginary damage equal to 36% of Welt's ATK to a single enemy and delivers two additional hits, each dealing Imaginary damage to a random enemy. There's a 65% base chance to reduce the enemy's SPD by 10% for 2 turns. The ultimate ability unleashes Imaginary damage equal to 90% of Welt's ATK to all enemies, with a 100% chance to imprison them for 1 turn, delaying their actions by 32% and reducing SPD by 10%. Welt's talent enables him to deal additional Imaginary damage equal to 30% of his ATK to already slowed enemies. The technique creates a dimension lasting 15 seconds, reducing enemies' Movement SPD by 50%. Upon entering battle in the dimension, there's a 100% base chance to imprison enemies for 1 turn, delaying their actions by 20% and reducing SPD by 10%. Only one dimension created by allies can exist simultaneously.

Luocha's Abilities

Luocha is a S rank sustain character. Luocha exhibits a healing-focused skill set with distinct abilities. His basic attack deals Imaginary damage equal to 50% of his ATK to a single enemy. The skill promptly restores the target ally's HP by 40% of Luocha's ATK plus 200, while granting Luocha a stack of Abyss Flower. If any ally's HP drops to 50% or less, an effect similar to Luocha's Skill is triggered for that ally (without consuming Skill Points), and this effect can recur every 2 turns. Luocha's ultimate removes one buff from all enemies and deals Imaginary damage equal to 120% of his ATK to all enemies, adding another stack of Abyss Flower for Luocha. His talent activates when Abyss Flower reaches 2 stacks, allowing Luocha to deploy a Field against the enemy. When an ally attacks an enemy in the Field, the attacking ally's HP is restored by 12% of Luocha's ATK plus 60. The Field's effect persists for 2 turns and is dispelled when Luocha is knocked down. Luocha's technique immediately triggers his Talent at the start of the battle.

Imbibitor Lunae's Abilities

Imbibitor Lunae is a A rank damage dealer. Imbibitor Lunae showcases a diverse array of abilities. His basic attack employs a 2-hit attack, dealing Imaginary damage equal to 50% of his ATK to a single enemy. Enhanced Basic Attack Levels, such as "Transcendence," "Divine Spear," and "Fulgurant Leap," offer progressively powerful attacks, with each level costing Skill Points. His Skill enhances the Basic ATK, allowing up to 3 consecutive enhancements that transform it into different forms. The Ultimate unleashes a 3-hit attack, dealing Imaginary damage equal to 180% of Imbibitor Lunae’s ATK to a single enemy, with additional damage to adjacent targets. It awards Squama Sacrosancta stacks, replaceable for Skill Points. Imbibitor Lunae's Talent grants Righteous Heart stacks, increasing his DMG by 5% per hit, stackable up to 6 times. His Technique triggers the Leaping Dragon state for 20 seconds, enabling rapid movement with attacks that deal Imaginary damage and block incoming attacks. Attacking enemies in this state activates Squama Sacrosancta and deals Imaginary damage to all enemies.

Clara's Abilities

Clara is a B rank damage dealer. Clara possesses a strategic set of abilities tailored for combat. Her basic attack deals Physical damage equal to 50% of her ATK to a single enemy. The skill, targeting all enemies, inflicts Physical damage equal to 60% of Clara's ATK to all and an additional 60% to enemies marked by Svarog with a Mark of Counter, removing all Marks after use. Clara's ultimate provides her with a 15% additional damage reduction after use, while also attracting enemy attacks for 2 turns. Furthermore, Svarog's Counter is enhanced, triggering immediately when an ally is attacked and increasing its damage multiplier by 96%. Adjacent enemies take 50% of the damage dealt to the primary target, and this enhanced Counter can activate twice. Clara's talent, under Svarog's protection, reduces damage taken by 10% and marks enemies attacking Clara with Svarog's Mark of Counter, retaliating with a Counter that deals Physical damage equal to 80% of Clara's ATK. Clara's technique initiates an immediate attack and increases her chances of being targeted by enemies for 2 turns upon entering battle.

Trailblazer (Physical)'s Abilities

Trailblazer Physical is a C rank damage dealer. The Physical Trailblazer boasts a versatile skill set. The base attack delivers physical damage equal to 50% of the Trailblazer's attack to a single enemy. The skill elevates this by dealing physical damage equal to 62% of the Trailblazer's attack to a single enemy and adjacent foes. The ultimate presents a choice between two modes: Mode 1 inflicts physical damage equal to 300% of the Trailblazer's attack to a single enemy, while Mode 2 deals physical damage equal to 180% of the Trailblazer's attack to a single enemy, accompanied by additional physical damage equal to 108% of the Trailblazer's attack to adjacent enemies. The talent introduces a stacking mechanism, increasing the Trailblazer's attack by 10% each time Weakness Break is inflicted on an enemy, with a maximum of two stacks. Lastly, the technique provides immediate healing to all allies, restoring 15% of their respective max HP.

Himeko's Abilities

Himeko is a C rank damage dealer. Himeko's abilities revolve around powerful Fire damage and strategic play. Her basic attack deals Fire damage equal to 50% of her ATK to a single enemy. The skill amplifies this, delivering Fire damage equal to 100% of Himeko's ATK to a single enemy and additional Fire damage equal to 40% of her ATK to adjacent enemies. The ultimate unleashes widespread destruction, dealing Fire damage equal to 138% of Himeko's ATK to all enemies, and she gains extra Energy for each defeated enemy. Himeko's talent triggers Charge points when inflicting Weakness Break on an enemy, reaching a maximum of 3 points. If fully charged, she performs a follow-up attack dealing Fire damage equal to 70% of her ATK to all enemies when an ally attacks. At the start of battle, she gains 1 point of Charge. The technique creates a dimension lasting 15 seconds, raising Fire damage taken by enemies by 10% for 2 turns when entering battle within it. Only one dimension created by allies can exist simultaneously.

Trailblazer (Fire)'s Abilities

Trailblazer Fire is a C rank sustain character. The Trailblazer employs a versatile set of abilities centered around Fire damage and defensive capabilities. The basic attack deals Fire damage equal to 50% of the Trailblazer's ATK to a single enemy, acquiring 1 stack of Magma Will. Upon accumulating 4 or more Magma Will stacks, the enhanced Basic ATK is triggered, dealing Fire damage equal to 90% of the Trailblazer's ATK to a single enemy and additional Fire damage equal to 36% of the Trailblazer's ATK to adjacent enemies. The skill boosts DMG Reduction by 40%, grants 1 stack of Magma Will, and has a 100% chance to Taunt all enemies for 1 turn. The ultimate deals Fire damage equal to 50% of the Trailblazer's ATK plus 75% of the Trailblazer's DEF to all enemies, automatically enhancing the next Basic ATK without consuming Magma Will. The talent accumulates Magma Will stacks when hit, enhancing the Basic ATK when reaching 4 stacks. Additionally, applying a Shield to all allies absorbs damage equal to 4% of the Trailblazer's DEF plus 20, lasting for 2 turns whenever the Basic ATK, Skill, or Ultimate is used. The technique, after activation, grants a Shield at the start of the next battle, absorbing damage equal to 30% of the Trailblazer's DEF plus 384 for 1 turn.

Topaz's Abilities

Topaz is a A rank damage dealer. Topaz and Numby present a synergistic set of abilities. Topaz's basic attack deals Fire damage equal to 50% of her ATK to a single enemy. Her skill inflicts Proof of Debt on a target, increasing their DMG received from follow-up attacks by 25%, and if no enemy has Proof of Debt, Numby, summoned at the start of battle, deals Fire damage equal to 75% of Topaz's ATK to a random target. The ultimate places Numby in the Windfall Bonanza! State, boosting its DMG multiplier and CRIT DMG, and advancing Numby's action when enemies with Proof of Debt are attacked. Numby leaves the state after 2 attacks. Topaz's talent continuously summons Numby, who launches follow-up attacks on Proof of Debt-afflicted targets and advances actions when those enemies are hit. The talent also summons Numby at the start of battle, and Numby automatically searches for treasures using Topaz's technique, regenerating 60 Energy for Topaz after the first attack in the next battle. Defeating enemies in the Simulated Universe earns bonus credits, up to 10,000 credits per day, and a chance to obtain Cosmic Fragments. If Topaz is downed, Numby disappears.

Bronya's Abilities

Bronya is a S tier amplifier. Bronya exhibits a supportive skill set. Her basic attack deals Wind damage equal to 50% of her ATK to a single enemy. The skill removes a debuff from a single ally, enabling them to take immediate action and increasing their DMG by 33% for 1 turn. If used on Bronya herself, she cannot take immediate action again. The ultimate significantly boosts all allies' ATK by 33% and increases their CRIT DMG for 2 turns. Bronya's talent advances her next action by 15% after using her basic attack. Her technique, when activated, enhances all allies' ATK by 15% at the start of the next battle for 2 turns. Overall, Bronya's abilities focus on dispelling debuffs, boosting allies' damage, and providing beneficial effects to enhance the team's performance.

Blade's Abilities

Blade is a B rank damage dealer. Blade's unique abilities strike a balance between offense and self-sacrifice. His basic attack inflicts Wind damage equal to 50% of his ATK on a single enemy. The enhanced basic attack, "Forest of Swords," consumes a portion of his Max HP but delivers potent Wind damage to the target and adjacent enemies, with the cost preventing Skill Points regeneration. His skill, "Hellscape," sacrifices 30% of his Max HP to enhance damage by 12%, transforming his basic attack into "Forest of Swords" for 3 turns. The ultimate resets Blade's current HP to 50% of his Max HP, dealing significant Wind damage to a single enemy and adjacent targets, capped at 90% of his Max HP loss. Blade's talent, "Charge," stacks with damage or HP consumption, triggering a follow-up attack on all enemies and restoring HP upon reaching maximum stacks. The technique, after an immediate attack, consumes 20% of Blade's Max HP to deal substantial Wind damage to all enemies. Blade's playstyle revolves around strategic HP management for increased damage and utilizing the Charge mechanic for impactful follow-up attacks.

Huohuo's Abilities

Huohuo is a S rank sustain character. Huohuo specializes in supportive and control abilities. Her basic attack inflicts Wind damage equivalent to 25% of her max HP on a target enemy. The skill dispels a debuff from a single ally, immediately restoring their HP and providing adjacent allies with healing based on percentages of Huohuo's max HP. Her ultimate replenishes Energy for all allies and enhances their ATK for 2 turns. Huohuo's talent, Divine Provision, triggers healing for allies at the start of their turn or upon using their ultimate, dispelling one debuff in the process and applying to allies with 50% HP or lower. The technique induces Horror-Struck on surrounding enemies, compelling them to flee from Huohuo for 10 seconds, and entering battle with Horror-Struck enemies reduces the ATK of every enemy by 25% for 2 turns. Huohuo's comprehensive abilities make her a valuable support character with healing, debuff removal, and crowd control attributes for the team.

Gepard's Abilities

Gepard is a A rank sustain character. Gepard's abilities focus on ice damage and defensive support. His basic attack deals Ice damage equal to 50% of his ATK to a single enemy. The skill inflicts significant Ice damage to a single enemy, with a chance to Freeze them for 1 turn, during which they take additional Ice damage at the beginning of each turn. Gepard's ultimate provides a shield to all allies, absorbing damage based on a percentage of his DEF plus a constant value for 3 turns. His talent ensures that when facing a killing blow, Gepard's HP is immediately restored to 25% of his Max HP, with this effect limited to once per battle. Additionally, Gepard's technique, when used, grants a shield to all allies at the start of the next battle, absorbing damage based on a percentage of his DEF plus a constant value for 2 turns. Overall, Gepard combines offensive freezing capabilities with strong defensive support for the team.

Yanqing's Abilities

Yanqing is a C rank damage dealer. Yanqing's abilities are tailored for ice damage and critical hits. His basic attack deals Ice damage equal to 50% of his ATK to a single enemy. The skill intensifies this, dealing Ice damage equal to 110% of Yanqing's ATK to a single enemy while activating Soulsteel Sync for 1 turn. The ultimate amplifies Yanqing's CRIT Rate by 60%, and when Soulsteel Sync is active, his CRIT DMG receives an additional 30%, lasting for one turn. Subsequently, the ultimate deals substantial Ice damage equal to 210% of Yanqing's ATK to a single enemy. Yanqing's talent makes him less likely to be targeted during Soulsteel Sync, increasing his CRIT Rate and CRIT DMG. After attacking, there's a fixed chance for a follow-up attack, dealing Ice damage and potentially Freezing the enemy for 1 turn. If Yanqing receives damage, the Soulsteel Sync effect vanishes. His technique, when used, enhances his damage output for 2 turns against enemies with 50% or higher current HP at the start of the next battle. Yanqing combines critical hits and freezing effects for a versatile and impactful playstyle.

Jingliu's Abilities

Jingliu is a S rank damage dealer. Jingliu's abilities revolve around ice damage and the unique mechanic of Syzygy. Her basic attack deals Ice damage equal to 50% of her ATK to a single enemy. The skill inflicts Ice damage equal to 100% of Jingliu's ATK to a single enemy and accumulates 1 stack of Syzygy. The enhanced skill consumes 1 stack of Syzygy, dealing increased Ice damage to the target and adjacent enemies without using Skill Points. Jingliu's ultimate delivers substantial Ice damage to a single enemy and adjacent foes, gaining 1 Syzygy stack after the attack. Her talent introduces the Spectral Transmigration state when Jingliu has 2 stacks of Syzygy, boosting her CRIT Rate and granting an Action Advance of 100%. In this state, her attacks consume HP from allies but significantly increase her ATK based on the total HP consumed. Syzygy can stack up to 3 times, depleting with each use and causing Jingliu to exit the Spectral Transmigration state. Her technique creates a dimension, freezing all enemies within it. Entering combat in this dimension grants Energy, a Syzygy stack, and a chance to Freeze enemy targets. The frozen enemies suffer additional Ice damage at the start of each turn. Jingliu's playstyle combines ice-based attacks, Syzygy mechanics, and strategic freezing effects.

Kafka's Abilities

Kafka is a B rank damage dealer. Kafka's abilities center around lightning damage and the infliction of the Shock status. Her basic attack delivers Lightning DMG equal to 50% of her ATK to a single enemy. The skill intensifies the lightning assault, dealing Lightning DMG to the target enemy and adjacent foes, with added damage to enemies currently under Damage over Time (DoT) effects. The ultimate unleashes a widespread Lightning DMG to all enemies, inducing the Shock status with additional damage from their current Shock state. Shocked enemies also endure Lightning DoT at the start of each turn. Kafka's talent triggers a follow-up attack after an ally's Basic ATK, dealing Lightning DMG and potentially inflicting Shock on the targeted enemy. This effect activates once per turn. Kafka's technique extends her lightning prowess, attacking all enemies within range and applying Shock to each target for a duration. Overall, Kafka excels in dealing lightning damage and strategically leveraging the Shock status for additional effects.

Jing Yuan's Abilities

Jing Yuan is a B rank damage dealer. Jing Yuan's abilities revolve around dealing Lightning damage and summoning Lightning-Lord as an auxiliary combat entity. His basic attack inflicts Lightning DMG ranging from 50% to 130% of his ATK on a single enemy. The skill unleashes Lightning DMG (50%–125% of Jing Yuan's ATK) on all enemies and augments Lightning-Lord's Hits Per Action by 2 for the subsequent turn. The ultimate escalates the Lightning DMG to all enemies (120%–240% of Jing Yuan's ATK) and boosts Lightning-Lord's Hits Per Action by 3 for the next turn. Jing Yuan's talent introduces Lightning-Lord at the battle's onset, which delivers follow-up attacks with Lightning DMG to a random single enemy and adjacent foes, dependent on Hits Per Action and SPD, capped at 10 Hits Per Action. If Jing Yuan is downed or affected by Crowd Control, the Lightning-Lord disappears. Additionally, Jing Yuan's technique enhances Lightning-Lord's Hits Per Action by 3 at the beginning of the next battle. This synergy allows Jing Yuan to harness Lightning-Lord's unique capabilities for strategic advantages in combat.

Bailu's Abilities

Bailu is a B rank sustain character. Bailu's abilities center around healing and providing invigoration to allies. His basic attack deals Lightning DMG equal to 50% of his ATK to a single enemy. The skill heals a single ally for 7.8% of Bailu's Max HP plus 78 and then heals random allies two more times, with diminishing HP restoration after each healing. The ultimate heals all allies for 9% of Bailu's Max HP plus 90, applying or extending Invigoration for 2 turns. Invigoration boosts allies and, when triggered by attacks, restores their HP. Bailu's talent ensures that allies with Invigoration won't be knocked down when receiving a killing blow, and he immediately heals them. This effect can activate twice, while the technique grants Invigoration to all allies at the start of the next battle for 2 turns. Bailu's kit emphasizes sustained healing and support for the team.
