Pricing

Individuals

The cost for one person going rafting will be 90$. You will be on a raft with other strangers if you do choose this price. Includes lifejacket, oars and various other rental equipment.

Groups(6 People)

The cost for a group of 6 will be 480$. Your group will get their own raft and tour guide. Includes lifejacket, oars, and various other rental equipment.
